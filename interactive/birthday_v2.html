Age Re-Unit-ed

If you have ever wondered how old you are in other units of time besides years — and, more importantly, if you are currently located in a time zone different from your place of birth, then use this tool to precisely know your age.

I once heard Neil deGrasse Tyson say on StarTalk that he celebrated his one-billionth second with a sip of champagne, which inspired me to add the tweak below. If you wonder when you will reach, say, 20,000 minutes of age, or 1.5 billion seconds, or 400 months — in short, any number and any unit of your choice — use the bottom panel to compute it.

This started out as a birthday gift for Basu. It took me more than a year to get it working, however, the great habit of birthdays of coming back around made it alright!
Tip: include the time if you know it; otherwise just use 00:00.
