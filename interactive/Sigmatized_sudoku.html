"SIGMA"tized rules

  1. Normal sudoku rules apply.
  2. Digits separated by a solid black dot are in a 1:2 ratio.
  3. Digits separated by a white dot are consecutive.
  4. Digits separated by a Roman numeral five sum to 5.
    • All such dominoes are given.
  5. Digits separated by a Roman numeral ten sum to 10.
    • Not all such dominoes are given.
  6. German whisper line (green line): Digits across a green line are at least 5 apart. If a green line spans two cells, then their difference is exactly 5. Digits on a German whisper line may repeat if allowed by sudoku rules.
  7. Renban line (purple line): digits on a purple line must form a consecutive, non-repeating set in any order.
  8. Digits on both diagonals do not repeat.
