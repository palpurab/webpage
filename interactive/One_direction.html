How this page works: Select one or more squares, then use the pad. # enters final digits. ✓ checks the grid. ✏️ makes pencil marks, 🎨 colors cells, and 🧽 erases. In pencil mode, keyboard digits or letters such as a, b, c add/remove pencil marks. Drag across cells or Shift/Ctrl-click to select several cells.

"Nine" realms rules

  1. Normal sudoku rules apply.
  2. Digits 1–9 do not repeat in each colored regions.
  3. Cages that have a dotted boundary are sum cages, and the sum value of those cages is denoted on the top left.
  4. Cells separated by a V sum to 5.
  5. Cages separated by a black dot have a 1:2 ratio among them.
  6. Cages separated by a white dot are consecutive.
